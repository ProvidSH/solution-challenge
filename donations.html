Water Sanctuary

Ways to Donate:

There are multiple methods on donating to water consumption and conservation organizations. They even have different ways for you to donate!


1. charity: water -- With a goal to end the global water crisis, charity: water serves as a bridge between fundraisers and donators. With over 90,000 water projects funded and over 14,000,000 people served, this organization strives for a better future.

2. Water First -- Based in Canada, Water First has a mission to bring indigenous communities safe and clean water. They deveop restoration projects, water quality studies, and even offer an internship program for those willing to help out on preserving water.

3. Just a Drop -- Just a Drop brings sustainable safe water, sanitation and hygiene projects to communities, which transform lives for many less economically developed countries (LEDCs). They believe that LEDCs deserve clean and safe water, and encourage everyone to donate their time and money for these communities in need.

4. Stockholm International Water Institute (SIWI) -- This not-for-profit institute provides knowledge, develops capacity, and offers policy advice to countries, communities, and companies who bring sustainable safe water, sanitation and hygiene projects to communities. SIWI has been transforming lives for many LEDCs through their wide range of expertise in water governance.

5. H2ouse -- Based in Indiana, H2ouse has focused on advocating for some of the best ways to save water. They have been providing knowledge on certain appliances that require water, and how citizens can find alternatives to preserve more water.
